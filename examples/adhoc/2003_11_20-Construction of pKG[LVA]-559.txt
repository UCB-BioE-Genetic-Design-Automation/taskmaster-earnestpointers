Construction of GFP[LVA] transposon,  General Scheme:
PCR RBS through T0 term, sub into XbaI/EcoRI sites
Subclone the XhoI/EcoRI fragment into pKD455-GFP
Later transfer into the DeLorenzo transposition plasmid

-->Construction of pKG[LVA]-559

PCR ca559F/R on pPROBE-GFP[LVA] (~970 bp)
Digest XbaI/EcoRI
Sub into XbaI/EcoRI sites of pKG437
	Cuts: 754 + 2013
	Product: 2926 bp

-->Construction of pKD455-GFP[LVA]

PCR with CA440F/ca559R on pKG[LVA]-559 (~1170 bp)
Digest XhoI/EcoRI
sub into XhoI/EcoRI sites of pKD455-GFP
	Cuts:  3490 (+ 670 + 298)
	Product: 4651 bp
